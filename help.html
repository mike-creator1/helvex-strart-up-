Frequently Asked
How do I create my first AI model?
Go to Model Builder, upload your training data, choose a base model, and click Fine-Tune. Your model will be ready in the Deployments tab once training completes.
What file formats does Knowledge Base support?
PDF, Word (.docx), Markdown (.md), plain text (.txt), and HTML files. Web URLs can be scraped and indexed directly from the Import Sources panel.
Can I connect my own API keys?
Yes. Go to Settings and add your own OpenAI, HelveX, or other provider keys. Your keys are encrypted at rest and never shared with other workspaces.
How does billing and credit usage work?
Each action — model inference, workflow run, website build — consumes credits. View your real-time balance and projected burn rate in the Billing section.
