Areas

What we work on.

Six concentrations across the team. The list isn't exhaustive — it's the work we believe matters most for the next era of capable, honest AI.

01

Reasoning

How models allocate thought, when they should slow down, and what makes a chain of reasoning trustworthy enough to act on.

02

Long-horizon agents

Memory hierarchies, plan critique, deterministic replay — the engineering of agents that hold their head over hours of work.

03

Evaluation

Measuring what matters. Benchmarks that map to actual outcomes, and the honest limits of what current evaluations can claim.

04

Safety & alignment

Refusals, red-teaming, failure mode taxonomies, and the engineering of guardrails that don't degrade the model into unhelpfulness.

05

Multimodal grounding

Reading documents, charts, diagrams, scans, and audio with the same reliability as well-formatted text. The hard half of "multimodal."

06

Retrieval & citation

Hybrid search, permission-aware retrieval, and how to make every claim traceable to the source it came from.
