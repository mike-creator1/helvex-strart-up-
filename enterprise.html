What we deliver

Five enterprise systems. All deployed in your cloud.

Enterprise AI systems

Strategic AI infrastructure: model routing, prompt governance, central observability, change-management workflows. The substrate that makes 50 individual AI projects coherent.

Internal AI assistants

Domain assistants for finance, HR, legal, ops — connected to the systems each team actually uses, with role-aware access from day one.

Team productivity AI

Embedded into the tools people already work in (Slack, Teams, email). Reduces switching cost, lifts adoption, makes ROI measurable.

Secure AI workflows

DLP-aware, audit-logged, role-scoped. Sensitive data stays where it's supposed to. PII is detected and handled before it touches a model.

Company knowledge systems (RAG)

Retrieval over the docs, wikis, and tickets your team already writes. With citation links so every answer can be traced to source.

Procurement & security review support
