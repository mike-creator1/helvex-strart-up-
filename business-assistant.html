How it works

Four steps. From brief to draft in minutes.

01

Profile

Set your business once

Company name, sector, jurisdiction, tone of voice, logo, bank/VAT details. Every future document inherits this without re-typing.

02

Choose

Pick a document type

Business plan, NDA, invoice, pitch deck, letter, email — or describe what you need in plain language and the assistant picks the template.

03

Brief

One paragraph is enough

Describe the situation in plain language. The assistant asks two or three follow-ups when it needs to, never more.

04

Send

Review, edit, export

Output lands in the editor. Edit anything inline. Export as Word, PDF, or Markdown. Or email it directly from the canvas.
