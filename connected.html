How linking works

Same account

One identity, many sign-ins

A connected provider lets you sign in to the same HelveX account — projects, billing, and team don't change. The provider is just a second front door.

Email match required

We never auto-merge

We only link a provider whose verified email matches the email on your HelveX account. Different email? Sign in with that provider creates a separate account.

Disconnect anytime

Unless it's your last one

We block disconnecting the only remaining sign-in method, since that would lock you out. Set a password first, then unlink.
