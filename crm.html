The CRM that
runs itself.

Clients, leads, pipeline, follow-ups, reminders — all on one surface, with an AI brain that surfaces the right contact at the right moment, and a sender that reaches them on email or WhatsApp.

What's inside

Seven surfaces. One source of truth.

Client management

Every contact, every conversation, every invoice in one record. Searchable by intent, not just by name.

Leads management

Capture from your site, sort by score, qualify with notes — and never drop a hot lead because the inbox swallowed it.

Sales pipeline

A drag-and-drop pipeline that updates itself when emails get sent and contracts get signed. The board reflects reality.

Automated follow-ups

Sequences that pause when a reply comes in, resume when it stalls, and adjust tone based on where the lead is in the funnel.

Email & WhatsApp on one screen

Unified thread per contact. Reply from either channel without leaving the record. History stays linked.

Smart reminders

"Call back next Tuesday" becomes a real task. The CRM nudges you before things go cold, not after.

AI customer insights

Patterns surface themselves. You just decide.

Hot now

Leads scored by signal strength — recency, channel, deal size — re-ranked every hour.

Going cold

Contacts you should reach today, surfaced before the trail dies. With a draft reply ready.

Pattern view

Which sources convert, which messaging works, which deal shapes win — without spreadsheets.

Smart digest

A morning brief: what's hot, what stalled, what closed. Written, not graphed.

How it works

Four steps. First contact in an afternoon.

01

Connect

Link your inbox & WhatsApp

Sign in with Gmail or Outlook. Connect your WhatsApp Business number. Imports run in the background.

02

Import

Contacts & deals from where you keep them

CSV, Google Contacts, Apple Contacts, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Notion — pulled in, deduplicated, mapped to fields you set.

03

Configure

Pipeline stages & sequences

Drag your deal stages, write your follow-up cadence, pick your reminder rules. Templates exist if you'd rather start from one.

04

Run

The CRM keeps itself current

Replies move deals. Calendar invites become events. The morning brief lands at 7am. You decide. The rest happens.
