How we work

Four steps. Six weeks. Written output.

01

Listen

Stakeholder interviews & data review

Two weeks of deep listening with the people who own the work. We read your existing docs, watch the workflows, talk to dissenters.

02

Diagnose

Where the leverage actually is

We rank opportunities by impact, effort, and reversibility. Cheap-and-fast wins surfaced separately from strategic bets.

03

Document

Written plan, executive-readable

A prioritised roadmap with owners, sequencing, dependencies, and kill criteria. The version that survives the room.

04

Hand off

Brief the team — and step back

We brief your team, answer the hard questions, and stay reachable. You decide whether to keep us involved past this point.
