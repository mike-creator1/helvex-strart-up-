HelveX · account recovery

Forgot your password?
It happens.

We'll send a 6-digit code to your registered email. Enter it on the next screen and choose a new password — your portal access stays paused until reset is complete.

  • Sixty-minute window. The reset code is good for 60 minutes from the moment you request it.
  • Sessions stay signed in. Your existing browser sessions remain active until you change your password.
  • Need a hand? Email hello@helvex.ch and a senior engineer will help you back in.
