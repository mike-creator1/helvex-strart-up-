Privacy

What we do with your data.

Effective Nov 12, 2026

Plainly: we don't train on your work. Anything you write or upload into HelveX is not used to improve our models, full stop. Enterprise workspaces add zero-retention mode that deletes inputs immediately after the response is delivered.

Data we collect

Account info, the prompts and files you bring, and operational logs needed to run the service. Nothing else.

How long we keep it

Defaults follow your plan. Enterprise sets retention per workspace, including zero-retention mode.

Where we keep it

In the region your workspace is pinned to: EU-Central (Frankfurt or Zurich), US-East, or APAC.

Who we share it with

Sub-processors needed to run the service (listed in the DPA). Never sold, never shared with advertisers.

Data subject requests — access, correction, deletion, portability — are handled within 30 days and free of charge. Email privacy@helvex.ai with the request and the workspace ID.

Terms of Service

The rules of using HelveX.

Effective Nov 12, 2026

Plainly: use HelveX for legitimate work, don't try to break it, pay for what you use, and we will keep the service running. The full legal terms expand each line; this section is the readable summary.

01 · Your content stays yours.

You own everything you bring in and everything the model returns to you. We get a narrow licence to operate the service on your behalf — nothing more.

02 · Acceptable use.

No deception, no harassment, no infringement, no use against export controls, no scraping behind authentication, no benchmarks designed to look like attacks. The full list lives in the formal terms.

03 · Plans and billing.

Prepaid by month or year. Overages, when they apply, are visible in the workspace before they invoice. Cancel any time; export your data first.

04 · Service levels.

Enterprise plans include a written SLA with uptime targets and a credit schedule. Pro plans run on best-effort with the public status page as the source of truth.

05 · Liability and indemnity.

Standard caps for SaaS. The formal terms govern; this summary does not.

06 · Governing law.

Swiss law, jurisdiction in Zurich. EU customers can elect EU law and venue in writing.

For the formal terms in full, write to legal@helvex.ai.

Security

How we protect the work.

Last reviewed Nov 2026

Plainly: encrypted in transit and at rest, region-pinned, single-tenant data plane available, audit logs to your SIEM, SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 in place, penetration tests twice a year by an independent firm.

Encryption

TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256 at rest, customer-managed keys for Enterprise.

Access control

SSO via SAML / OIDC, SCIM provisioning, role-based access at every surface.

Network

VPC isolation, IP allow-list, private connectivity over PrivateLink for Enterprise.

Audit logging

Per-action logs exported to S3, GCS, or Splunk-compatible SIEMs.

Compliance

SOC 2 Type II · ISO 27001 · ISO 27701 · GDPR · CCPA · HIPAA-ready.

Vulnerability mgmt

Continuous scanning, two external pen-tests per year, public bug bounty.

Report a vulnerability to security@helvex.ai. Fixes for high-severity findings ship within five business days.

DPA

Data Processing Agreement.

v3 · effective Nov 12, 2026

Plainly: we are a processor on the personal data you bring in. We process it only on your instructions, only for the purpose of running HelveX, with sub-processors disclosed in writing and binding flow-down obligations on each one.

  • ▸
    Standard Contractual Clauses (EU 2021/914) for transfers outside the EEA, plus the UK addendum.
  • ▸
    Sub-processor list updated 30 days before any change. Right of objection in writing.
  • ▸
    Breach notification within 72 hours of becoming aware, with the facts as we have them.
  • ▸
    Audit rights on reasonable notice; SOC 2 Type II report satisfies most enterprise audits without on-site visit.
  • ▸
    Deletion on termination within 30 days, or sooner on request, with written confirmation.
