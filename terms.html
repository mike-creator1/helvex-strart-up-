Terms

The rules
of using HelveX.

Written so a non-lawyer can read them, with the formal terms linked from each section. Effective Nov 12, 2026.

In one sentence: use HelveX for legitimate work, do not try to break it, pay for what you use, and we will keep the service running. Below is each part of that promise, in writing.

01 · Your content stays yours.

You own everything you bring in and everything the model returns to you. We get a narrow licence to operate the service on your behalf — nothing more.

02 · Acceptable use.

No deception, no harassment, no infringement, no use against export controls, no scraping behind authentication, no benchmarks designed to look like attacks. The full list lives in the formal terms.

03 · Plans and billing.

Prepaid by month or year. Overages, when they apply, are visible in the workspace before they invoice. Cancel any time; export your data first.

04 · Service levels.

Enterprise plans include a written SLA with uptime targets and a credit schedule. Pro plans run on best-effort with the public Status page as the source of truth.

05 · Liability and indemnity.

Standard caps for SaaS. The formal terms govern; this summary does not.

06 · Termination.

You can cancel any time, with data export available for 90 days afterward. We can terminate for material breach, with notice and a chance to cure.

07 · Governing law.

Swiss law, jurisdiction in Zurich. EU customers can elect EU law and venue in writing.

08 · Disputes.

Good-faith negotiation first. If that fails, mediation before arbitration. Class waivers do not apply where the law forbids them.

For the formal terms in full, write to legal@helvex.ai.
