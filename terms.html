§ 03

Services description

HelveX provides three principal categories of Service: model inference, workspace surfaces, and managed engagements. This section describes each at a level sufficient to inform the Customer's procurement and use; the technical detail of each is set out in product documentation.

Model inference

HelveX operates three production models. Ether is the real-time tier, optimised for low latency and high throughput. Nexus is the balanced reasoning tier, with the longest context window in the family. Prometheus is the frontier reasoning tier, including agentic capabilities. Customer accesses model inference through the API surface and through the workspace UI. The capabilities, intended use cases, and known limitations of each model are described in the model card published in the Trust Center.

Workspace surfaces

The workspace comprises six surfaces: Chat for conversational interaction; Projects for grouping conversations, files, and instructions under a shared purpose; Agents for autonomous task execution; Knowledge for retrieval-augmented use of Customer documents; Spaces for collaborative workspaces shared across teams; and History for replayable audit and retrieval of past interactions.

HelveX's senior-engineer practice delivers managed engagements under separate Order Forms.
