Fact sheet

The on-the-record version.

Six facts that can be quoted as-is. Up to date as of the current cycle; refresh dates posted in the trust ribbon below.

  • Founded 2024, Zurich, Switzerland. Private company, Swiss-incorporated as HelveX AG.
  • HQ Zurich, Switzerland. Engineering distributed across EU, UK, and North America.
  • Models Ether (real-time, lowest cost), Nexus (reasoning, long context), Prometheus (agentic, computer use).
  • Workspace surfaces Chat, Projects, Agents, Knowledge, Spaces, History. One operating layer across all three models.
  • Engagement model Senior engineer per engagement. Scope → Architect → Build → Operate. Written briefs, weekly ship cadence, monthly retainer.
  • Deployment posture SaaS by default. Enterprise plans run inside the customer's AWS or GCP tenant. SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, GDPR.
