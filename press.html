Brand assets

Logos, type, colour tokens.

Everything you need to set HelveX in print or on screen. The full brand book lives on a dedicated page — colour values, typography pairings, and the rules around the wordmark and the X. For asset files outside the kit, write to brand.

Open brand book Email brand@helvex.ai

Wordmark

Capital H, lowercase elve, capital X. The X carries the red brand colour. Never recoloured, never lowercased, never stretched.

Type pairing

Inter for body and headings; Instrument Serif italic for editorial accents and pull-lines. Inter weights 300–900 are all in use.

Colour tokens

Ink #0A0A0A, red #DC2626, amber #F59E0B, cream #FAFAF7. Full token table on the brand page.

Voice

Methodical, plain English, no AI hyperbole. We do not write "revolutionary", "world-class", or "industry-first". We describe what the product does.
