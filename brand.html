Brand

draw us, name us.

A few rules so the brand stays consistent wherever it shows up. Ask brand@helvex.ai if anything is unclear.

Wordmark

HelveX

Always written as one word. Capital H, lowercase elve, capital X. The X carries the brand colour — never substituted, never lowercased, never recoloured to match a partner's palette.

Pronunciation

Brand system

The full visual system.

The wordmark, the type pairing, the colour palette, and the voice. Together these make a page read as HelveX even when the logo is not in view.

Wordmark · TECH caption

HelveX

Tech

The wordmark sits above a small caption — typically "TECH" — set in monospaced, wide-tracked uppercase. The caption locks to the bottom of the wordmark on its leftmost letter and never sets larger than one quarter of the wordmark cap-height.

Voice

Methodical. Plain-English. No AI hyperbole.

We describe what the product does, in the words the reader would use. We do not write "revolutionary", "world-class", "industry-first", or "game-changing". We do not write "leverage" when we mean "use". We do not bury a hedge in a footnote.

If a sentence could appear on any other AI platform's site without changing meaning, it does not belong on ours.

Logo usage

Do this. Don't do that.

A short list of the choices we make every time we set the wordmark — and the ones we never make.

Do

  • ·Keep at least one wordmark cap-height of clear space on every side.
  • ·Set the X in red #DC2626, ink for the rest of the letters.
  • ·On dark surfaces, set the body of the wordmark in white and keep the red X.
  • ·Use the wordmark at no smaller than 24 px tall on screen, 20 mm in print.
  • ·Pair with the small TECH caption only when there is space — never crammed.

Don't

  • ·Recolour the wordmark to match a partner's palette.
  • ·Stretch, skew, or condense the wordmark on any axis.
  • ·Lowercase the X, or set it in any colour other than red on a light surface.
  • ·Place the wordmark on a photographic background without an ink wash.
  • ·Reproduce the wordmark in any font other than the supplied vector.
